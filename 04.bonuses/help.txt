
Resume / CV Pack


Description
===========

High Quality, print ready (CMYK) Resume / CV Pack that includes: Resume template, Letter, Envelope and a Business card.


Template Size
=============


Sizes are: 

- Resume template: 2 A4 pages 
- Letter: A4 
- Envelope: No. 10 standard
- Business card: 9 * 5 cm.


Fonts Used
==========

The fonts used in the templates include:
	
	- Open Sans: http://www.google.com/fonts#UsePlace:use/Collection:Open+Sans

Usage
=====

Open the PSD files with Adobe Photoshop CS6.

The files are in CMYK mode and 300dpi format.

The files are divided into logical sections and you just have to insert your text / duplicate sections if needed.

For every picture from the theme there is a shape (named image bg shape) and you just have to make a clipping mask
 between the picture and the shape.
